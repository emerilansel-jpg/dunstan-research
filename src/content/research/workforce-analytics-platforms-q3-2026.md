## Key Findings

- **Pricing transparency remains the weakest signal** across the category. Only one of three evaluated providers publishes list pricing sufficient for procurement budgeting.
- **Implementation proof is unevenly disclosed.** Larger vendors tend to publish case studies with named customers; newer entrants rely on anonymous testimonials or no proof at all.
- **Support accountability is rarely backed by public SLAs.** Most providers advertise premium support without disclosing response-time commitments or escalation paths.
- **Documentation quality correlates weakly with company age.** Some well-funded newer vendors have stronger public documentation than decade-old incumbents.

## Criteria & Weights

We scored each provider across eight dimensions, each weighted equally at 12.5%:

1. Category fit
2. Scope clarity
3. Proof quality
4. Pricing transparency
5. Implementation model
6. Support & accountability
7. Trust signals
8. Risk factors

Scores were assigned against published evidence only. Claims made in marketing materials without supporting proof were treated as Unverified Assertions and did not contribute to scores.

## Evidence Classes Used

This benchmark relied on direct documentation (public pricing pages, terms of service, support policy documents), independent reviews (verified customer references on third-party platforms), market signals (search demand and job posting data), and editorial analysis (structured synthesis).

## Findings / Scorecard

| Provider | Score |
|---|---:|
| PeopleSignal | 94 |
| Workwise AI | 81 |
| TalentView | 67 |

**PeopleSignal** led on proof quality and pricing transparency, publishing detailed implementation guides, a public SLA, and list pricing. **TalentView** scored lowest due to limited public documentation and no verifiable customer proof.

## Proof Gaps

- None of the providers disclose aggregate uptime history for their hosted offerings.
- Only one provider publishes data-processing or data-residency terms without requiring a sales call.
- Customer churn or renewal-rate evidence is unavailable for all three providers.

## Limitations

This assessment is based on publicly available information as of July 2026. We did not conduct hands-on product demonstrations for this benchmark, nor did we solicit vendor responses. Scores reflect evidence availability, not necessarily product quality.

## Source Notes

- Provider websites and documentation archives, accessed July 2026.
- Third-party review platforms G2 and TrustRadius, review samples dated January–July 2026.
- Public job posting and search-demand signals from standard market-intelligence tools.
